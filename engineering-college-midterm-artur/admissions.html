Admissions Overview

Join Engineering College and start building your future today. Our admissions process is designed to be clear, accessible, and supportive at every stage.

How to Apply

  1. Submit Your Application: Complete the online Registration Form with your personal and academic details.
  2. Attend the Interview: Shortlisted applicants will be invited for an interview (online or on campus).
  3. Receive Admission Decision: Successful candidates will get an official acceptance letter via email.
  4. Confirm Enrollment: Pay your tuition deposit to secure your place.

Required Documents
